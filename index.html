DEF JAM RECORDINGS BRAND GUIDELINES

Def Jam Recordings is the definitive and pioneering label in hip-hop, helping develop the careers of legendary artists like The Beastie Boys, LL Cool J, Kanye West, and Justin Bieber. With a storied history, and an ambitious future, but no official brand guidelines, the label came to Doubleday & Cartwright looking to help define the brand for the first time in an official guidelines document distributed to employees and artists. We developed a comprehensive graphic system, iconography, and poster series in order to do so.

Rules

Logo

  • Logo must be a wordmark
  • Record player icon must be on the right of the wordmark
  • Invert colors from black to white in response to darker and lighter backgrounds
  • Logo variations are color changes such as: white, black, yellow, and a solid outline border
  • The wordmark and icon are the prefered usage for partnerships, co-branding, and in media articles

A logo is a graphic mark, emblem, symbol, or stylized name used to identify a company, organization, product, or brand. It may take the form of an abstract or figurative design, or it may present as a stylized version of the company's name if it has sufficient brand recognition.
